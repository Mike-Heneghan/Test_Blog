Background

Growing Up

I was born in Edinburgh 10/03/1992 and grew up in Musselburgh, a town on the coast in East Lothian. Musselburgh is famous for it's racecourse and not an awful lot else.

University Years

After completing Secondary School I moved to Aberdeen to undertake a fast track masters in Mechanical and Offshore Engineering at the Robert Gordon University. It was here I had the opportunity to meet lots of new people and cultivate hobbies and interests. In particular the indoor climbing wall and snowsports dry slope.

Professional Life

After completing my studies I joined Schluberger Oilfield Services as a Completions Field Engineer. In this role I installed high accuracy downhole pressure/temrperature sensors in Oil and Gas Wells. I enjoyed the excitement of working in diverse places such as the North Sea and Malayasia as well as the training schools in Paris and Abu Dhabi. I also had thedubous honour of being featured in Petro Global News with a photo used from a brochure.

Hobbies

One of my favourite weekend past times is making the most of the Scottish country side by going hillwalking. I'm slowly making my way through all 283 Munroes and hope to have walked over 50 of them by the end of the year.

Indoor Climbing

Indoor climbing has become a particularly rewarding past time for me. In the last year I've become increasingly hooked on trying new routes and moves. I'm hoping to continue improving and try outdoor climbing in the summer.
